1. Discussion Guide
•	Main goal of the interview: Interaction with single or multiple consumers Independently 
•	Target audience: Any one in Sri Lanka
•	List of questions (in order):
•	Questions will differ based on the insights trying to get
•	•  Behavioral insights → habits, usage occasions, routines 
•	•  Decision journey → why they choose Brand A vs B 
•	•  Pain points → unmet needs (very valuable) 
•	•  Perception tracking → brand image, trust, quality 
•	•  Concept testing → reactions to new ideas/products

If it is a behavioural study then below are the questions (Like wise check for others from AI)
. Daily Routine & Context (Understand real-life usage)
•	“Can you walk me through your typical day from morning to night?” 
•	“At what times do you usually use [product category]?” 
•	“Where are you usually when you use it? (home, work, outside)” 
•	“Who else is involved when you use this product?” 
👉 Insight: Usage occasions, context, influence of others
________________________________________
2. Last Usage (Most powerful section – anchor on reality)
•	“When was the last time you used [product]?” 
•	“What exactly happened from start to finish?” 
•	“What made you decide to use it at that moment?” 
•	“What product did you use specifically?” 
•	“How did you feel after using it?” 
👉 AI Probe:
•	“Was this your usual routine or something different?” 
👉 Insight: Real behavior vs claimed behavior
________________________________________
3. Habit Formation (Understand patterns)
•	“How long have you been using this type of product?” 
•	“Is this something you do daily, weekly, or occasionally?” 
•	“What triggers you to use it?” 
•	“Are there times you skip using it? Why?” 
👉 Insight: Habit strength + triggers + drop-off points
________________________________________
4. Purchase Behaviour (Critical for FMCG)
•	“When did you last buy this product?” 
•	“Where did you buy it from?” 
•	“What made you choose that store?” 
•	“What options did you consider before buying?” 
•	“Did you plan the purchase or was it spontaneous?” 
👉 AI Probe:
•	“What almost made you choose something else?” 
👉 Insight: Decision journey + channel behavior
________________________________________
5. Brand Choice Behaviour
•	“Which brand did you buy last time?” 
•	“Why did you choose that brand?” 
•	“Have you switched brands recently?” 
•	“What made you switch?” 
👉 AI Probe:
•	“What would make you switch again?” 
👉 Insight: Loyalty vs switching triggers
________________________________________
6. Decision Drivers (Real vs claimed)
•	“What are the top things you look for when choosing this product?” 
•	“Which matters more: price, quality, or availability?” 
•	“Have you ever chosen a cheaper option instead? Why?” 
👉 AI Probe:
•	“Tell me about a time when you compromised” 
👉 Insight: Trade-offs (very important)
________________________________________
7. Barriers & Pain Points
•	“What do you find frustrating about this product/category?” 
•	“Have you ever had a bad experience?” 
•	“What problems do you face when buying or using it?” 
👉 AI Probe:
•	“How did you solve that issue?” 
👉 Insight: Unmet needs (innovation goldmine)
________________________________________
8. Workarounds & Alternatives (Hidden behavior)
•	“If this product is not available, what do you do?” 
•	“Do you ever use something else instead?” 
•	“Have you created your own solution?” 
👉 Insight: Substitution behavior + innovation opportunities
________________________________________
9. Social & Influence Behaviour
•	“Do you usually decide yourself or does someone influence you?” 
•	“Have you ever tried a product because of someone else?” 
•	“Do you recommend products to others?” 
👉 Insight: Word-of-mouth power
________________________________________
10. Occasional vs Regular Users
•	“Are there times you use this product more than usual?” 
•	“What situations increase your usage?” 
•	“When do you use it less?” 
👉 Insight: Demand spikes + seasonality
________________________________________
11. Emotional & Functional Outcome
•	“What does a ‘good experience’ with this product look like?” 
•	“How do you feel when it works really well?” 
•	“What makes it disappointing?” 
👉 Insight: Emotional drivers (important for branding)
________________________________________
12. Future Behaviour Signals
•	“Do you think your usage will change in the future?” 
•	“What might make you use more or less?” 
•	“Is there anything new you want to try?” 
👉 Insight: Forward-looking trends

•	Preferred probing style (e.g., deep, casual, short follow-ups):Casual but should be deep
